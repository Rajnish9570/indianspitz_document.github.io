Indian Spitz

The Indian Spitz is a medium size dog breed with long fur. If you are someone who lives in a small space like one room house, small apartment or flat, this breed cab be an ideal pet for your house. They are a clean breed and require minimal care. This breed is friendly and gets along well with kids and other animals.They are easy to train and can taught tricks quite easily.
